# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 31, 2026_

Parley is designed so your data never leaves your device. This policy describes exactly what happens to the audio, transcripts, and summaries Parley creates.

## What we collect from you

**Nothing.** Parley does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers. There are no Parley servers.

## What stays on your device

When you record audio in Parley, the following are stored only on your iPhone and encrypted with a 256-bit AES key that never leaves the device:

- Audio files
- Transcripts produced by on-device speech recognition (WhisperKit, running on Apple's Neural Engine)
- Speaker identification results (SpeakerKit, running on-device)
- Summaries, decisions, and action items generated by Apple Intelligence (Apple's on-device language model)
- Any names you assign to speakers

The encryption key is stored in the iOS Keychain with the `WhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly` accessibility tier, which excludes it from iCloud Keychain sync and from unencrypted backups.

## Network activity

Parley makes exactly one type of network request: on first launch it downloads the speech recognition and speaker identification model files from a public open-source model repository (Hugging Face). This download contains generic model weights only — none of your audio, transcripts, or summaries are transmitted. After this one-time download, Parley operates entirely offline.

## Permissions

- **Microphone** — required to record audio. The system prompt appears the first time you tap the record button. Audio capture happens only when you start a recording.
- **Face ID / Touch ID** — used to lock the app so that another person with access to your unlocked phone cannot view your recordings. Authentication happens entirely on-device via Apple's LocalAuthentication framework; Parley never sees your biometric data.

## No analytics, no tracking

Parley contains no analytics SDKs, no telemetry, no crash reporting services, and no advertising identifiers.

## Sharing

When you tap the share icon on a recording, Parley hands the formatted text to the standard iOS share sheet. From there, you choose which destination (Mail, Messages, Notes, etc.) receives the content. Parley itself does not share data with anyone.

## Children's privacy

Parley does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is not directed at children under 13.
